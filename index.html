Who We Are

About GPUCore Pro

A Bristol-based financial information consultancy trusted by institutions worldwide for integrity, precision, and actionable intelligence.

Building Trust Since 2009

GPUCore Pro was founded in Bristol with a singular focus: to bridge the gap between raw financial data and executive decision-making. What began as a boutique research desk has evolved into a full-spectrum information consultancy serving asset managers, banks, and corporate treasury teams across Europe and beyond.

Our analysts combine buy-side experience with academic rigour, ensuring every deliverable meets institutional standards for accuracy, timeliness, and compliance.

Global Reach, Local Roots

Headquartered at 98 Corn St in the heart of Bristol's financial district, we maintain a hybrid delivery model — on-site workshops, secure digital portals, and 24/7 alert systems tailored to each client's workflow.

Our Mission

To deliver unbiased, decision-ready financial intelligence that empowers our clients to navigate complexity with confidence and clarity.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted information partner for institutional investors seeking excellence in research quality, ethical standards, and innovation.

Our Promise

Every engagement is governed by strict confidentiality protocols, independent analysis, and transparent methodology documentation.

Our Approach

We integrate quantitative models with qualitative expert judgment — never relying on single-source data without rigorous validation.
